Our Story

Born from a passion for authentic Italian traditions, Crust & Co started as a humble idea between friends. We believe that great pizza requires only three things: simple ingredients, intense heat, and a lot of heart.

Our dough is fermented for 48 hours and topped with ingredients sourced directly from local farmers and Italian artisans. Every pie is baked in our custom-built wood-fired oven, resulting in that perfectly blistered, airy crust.
